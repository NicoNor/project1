About Us

Get to know the bakers that bring you joy.

Sweet Blossoms is a bakery that was founded in 2015 with the help of Chris Vincze, whom made Nico Noriega create a fictional webpage. Here at Sweet Blossoms our goal is to create the most visually stunning baked goods with a taste that you won't forget. With our weekly offers and constant change in pastry selection we bring a diverse selection and a reason for you to keep checking in!
